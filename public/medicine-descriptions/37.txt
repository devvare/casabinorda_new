
 ZOVIRAX FORTE (Aciclovir) 400 mg/100 ml Suspension – PRODUCT INFORMATION

 Overview

ZOVIRAX FORTE 400 mg/5 ml oral suspension is a potent antiviral medication used to treat and prevent a range of viral infections caused by Herpes simplex virus (HSV) and Varicella zoster virus (VZV). Its active substance, aciclovir, is a nucleoside analogue that selectively inhibits viral DNA polymerase, thereby disrupting viral replication. This suspension is administered orally and formulated to deliver high concentrations of the drug in a palatable liquid form, suitable for both adults and pediatric patients.

 Indications

ZOVIRAX FORTE is indicated for:
- Treatment of herpes labialis (cold sores), genital herpes, and herpetic skin infections
- Treatment of chickenpox (varicella) and shingles (herpes zoster)
- Prevention of recurrent herpes infections in immunocompetent individuals
- Prophylaxis in immunocompromised patients such as those with AIDS or undergoing bone marrow transplantation
- Neonatal and infant HSV infection support in coordination with IV therapy where appropriate

---

 Dosage and Administration

 Adults and Adolescents
- Herpes labialis: 200 mg (half dose) five times daily, every 4 hours, skipping the night dose, for 5 days.
- Suppression of HSV: 200 mg four times daily, or 400 mg twice daily.
- Prophylaxis in immunocompromised patients: 400 mg (1 full dose) four times daily.
- Chickenpox/Shingles: 800 mg (2 full doses) five times daily for 7 days.

 Pediatrics
- Children over 6 years: 800 mg (2 doses) 4 times a day
- 2–6 years: 400 mg (1 dose) 4 times a day
- Under 2 years: 200 mg (half dose) 4 times a day
- Doses can also be based on weight (20 mg/kg), up to a maximum of 800 mg/dose

 Elderly
- Due to decreased renal function, dose adjustment may be required.
- Ensure adequate hydration during treatment.

 Renal Impairment
- For herpes: 200 mg twice daily
- For varicella/zoster: 800 mg twice daily
- In moderate impairment: adjust to three doses per day instead of five

Administration:  
- Shake well before use.  
- Administer using the provided measuring device.  
- Do not skip or double doses.

---

 Contraindications

Do not use ZOVIRAX FORTE if:
- You are allergic to aciclovir, valaciclovir, or any excipients
- You have experienced severe allergic reactions such as swelling of the face or lips, hives, or difficulty breathing

---

 Warnings and Precautions

- Adequate fluid intake is crucial, especially in high-dose regimens
- Neurological side effects (e.g., dizziness, confusion) may occur in elderly or renally impaired patients
- Prolonged use in severely immunocompromised patients may lead to resistance
- Contains sorbitol; caution in patients with hereditary fructose intolerance

---

 Interactions

While ZOVIRAX has few significant interactions, some drugs may influence its metabolism or clearance:
- Probenecid and cimetidine may increase aciclovir levels
- Mycophenolate mofetil may have an additive effect
- Monitoring is advisable when co-administered with nephrotoxic agents

There is no need to adjust the dose in most cases, but physicians should consider the total pharmacological load.

---

 Side Effects

 Common:
- Headache
- Nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, or diarrhea
- Rash, itching, or skin irritation
- Tiredness or fever

 Uncommon:
- Urticaria (hives)
- Hair thinning

 Rare:
- Angioedema (swelling of face, lips, or throat)
- Elevated liver enzymes or bilirubin

 Very Rare:
- Blood disorders (e.g., anemia, thrombocytopenia)
- Hallucinations, psychosis
- Seizures or tremors
- Hepatitis, jaundice
- Kidney dysfunction or renal pain

If any severe reaction occurs, stop taking the medication and seek medical attention immediately.

---

 Overdose

Repeated high doses may result in gastrointestinal or neurological symptoms. In such cases:
- Symptomatic support is recommended
- Hemodialysis can significantly reduce serum aciclovir levels

---

 Storage and Handling

- Store between 15–25°C
- Keep away from children
- Discard any unused suspension after the expiration date
- Do not dispose of via wastewater; follow environmental regulations
